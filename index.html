Our Mission

There is food for everyone on this planet, but not everyone eats. ( by Carlo Petrini )

Our goals are

Avert Food Wastage

Today's wastage is tommorrow's shortage.Understanding this fact is the need of the hour, and through our approach we ought to do it.

Make sure - good food reaches to people

Often people throw edibles even after paying good money for them. We, through this initiative cash in on this fact , so that food can reach to those in need.

Control Organic Waste

Through our actions we tend to reduce the already increasing carbon footprint, thus contributing towards reduction of supervising greenhouse gases.

Develop Homogenity in Food Standards

By circulating the leftover nutrient rich food among the needies, we reduce the food quality gap among various sections of society.

Prediction and Analysis based results

Using advanced technologies to get the accurate results regarding food consumption and wastage.

Making Cost-Fit Food

Analysing the patterns in food consumption and wastage, the college messes can ameliorate the menus accordingly and aftermath food fees can be fixed, transforming food prone to wastage into cost-fit food.

About us

Our idea is an initiative working towards eliminating food waste. We started working towards our mission since last year, and till now we have saved and served approximately 8400 meals from getting wasted. We have also been able to reduce the ever increasing wastage of food through our intervention of saving the cooked food from going into bins.Our supporter colleges have helped us move closer to our mission of reducing food wastage, and all the stakeholders have been putting their efforts towards the realization of our vision of positioning India as a "Zero Food Wastage Economy" in near future. We are dedicated to ensure that no food is being wasted in India, and this will only be possible through our determination and the determination of all the members of the society.

We, through the help and support of our college, collect surplus unused food from college hostels' messes, schools, company warehouses, and households through our NutBit team. The food collected is then distributed to the people at the bottom of the pyramid, who put in hard day’s labour and are still not able to afford two square meals a day. We are currently operational in the city of Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh.
